Cooking Classes

With Christie and Chris's Cooking Cabin, we offer cooking classes for new and amature cooks. Each course ranging in the amount of time and rigor you want to put into it.
No matter if you your cooking skills currently consist of grilled cheese and ramen noodles. Every skill level is wanted and welcome. We encourage you to get out of your
comfort zone and pick up a new life long skill, that you will always be able to use. Come out of these cooking classes feeling more comfortable and confident with your cooking abilities.





Feel free to email us with any questions!


Benefits from cooking classes
  • 1. Gain the knowledge and skills in the world of cooking
  • 2. The ability to ask questions from highly skilled local chefs/cooks
  • 3. Use high quality kitchen equitment
  • 4. Become a more confident cook, a skill that you will always need
  • 5. Learn what can be a difficult skill, in a fun and friendly setting
