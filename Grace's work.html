Meet the Team

Our cooks have experiences all across the board. From beginners to advanced, our chefs will help you improve your cooking skills! The classes are designed for what you want to learn. They're perfect for families, clubs, and more! Come with your favorite recipe and come hungry!



Christie and Chris

These two lovebirds met in culinary school and took their joy of teaching and cooking to make this cooking cabin! They founded this cooking school with the heart of family and good food in mind. Christie and Chris believe the heart of family starts at the kitchen table. Which is why all classes are open to people of all ages!
